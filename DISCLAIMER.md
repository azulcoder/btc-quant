# Disclaimer — read this first

**This is a research and backtesting tool. It is NOT financial advice, and it does not place trades.**

- Nothing here is a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any asset.
- It places **no orders** and holds **no API keys**. There is zero execution and zero financial risk from the software itself.
- **A backtest is not a forecast.** Historical performance — however good — does not predict future returns. It is trivially easy to produce a beautiful backtest that loses money live (overfitting / selection bias). This tool deliberately reports **deflated Sharpe**, out-of-sample / walk-forward results, transaction costs, and turnover precisely so you can see through a flattering in-sample curve.
- **Edges decay.** Any documented anomaly is crowded and arbitraged over time. Treat every signal as a hypothesis to be re-tested, not a truth.
- Markets — crypto especially — carry the risk of total loss, exchange failure, and tail events a backtest never saw.
- You are solely responsible for any decision you make. Do your own research; consider consulting a licensed professional.

The intellectually honest stance of a real quant is *skepticism*: assume your edge is noise until costs, out-of-sample testing, and a deflated Sharpe say otherwise.
